Mould cleaning

Mould in your home or business can be unsightly and pose serious health risks, impacting indoor air quality and potentially causing respiratory issues. Our professional mould cleaning services are designed to identify, remove, and prevent mould growth, ensuring a safe, clean, and healthy environment. Our trained team uses industry-grade equipment and safe, effective methods to eliminate mould at its source and prevent it from returning.

Comprehensive Mould Cleaning Solutions

We understand that every mould situation is unique, which is why we offer tailored mould cleaning solutions for residential and commercial properties. From inspection to prevention, we ensure thorough and effective mould treatment that gives you peace of mind.

Mould Inspection and Assessment

Our mould inspection services help identify the extent and source of mould growth. We conduct a detailed assessment to determine the best treatment method for your space. Our inspection services include:

  • Visual Inspection: A thorough examination of affected areas to locate visible signs of mould and moisture sources.
  • Air Quality Testing: We test indoor air quality to detect airborne mould spores, giving us insight into hidden mould growth.
  • Moisture Detection: Using moisture meters, we identify damp areas that could encourage mould growth.

Mould Removal and Cleaning

Our mould removal process is designed to eliminate existing mould and clean affected areas safely and effectively. Our mould removal services include:

  • Containment and Safety Measures: We set up containment barriers to prevent mould spores from spreading during the cleaning process.
  • Surface Cleaning and Disinfection: We use specialized cleaning solutions to remove mould from surfaces and disinfect the area.
  • HEPA Filtration: High-efficiency particulate air (HEPA) filtration systems capture airborne mould spores, improving indoor air quality.

Mould Prevention and Maintenance

Preventing mould from returning is key to maintaining a safe and clean environment. Our mould prevention services include:

  • Moisture Control Recommendations: We provide guidance on controlling humidity and moisture to prevent mould growth.
  • Ventilation Improvement: Recommendations to improve airflow in areas prone to dampness, reducing the likelihood of mould.
  • Anti-Mould Treatments: We apply anti-mould coatings and treatments to vulnerable surfaces, protecting them from future mould growth.
