Bond cleaning

At Boostup Cleaning Services, we understand that moving out can be a stressful experience. With so many tasks to manage, from packing to arranging logistics, ensuring that the property is left spotlessly clean for a bond return is often overwhelming. Our professional bond cleaning services provide a complete, worry-free solution designed to meet the highest standards set by landlords and property managers, making sure you get your full bond back.

What Is Bond Cleaning and Why Is It Essential?

Bond cleaning, also known as end-of-lease cleaning, is an intensive cleaning service specifically tailored to fulfill the strict requirements for a rental property handover. Unlike regular cleaning, bond cleaning involves a comprehensive top-to-bottom scrub of every area of the property, leaving it in impeccable condition. Most landlords and property managers expect this deep level of cleanliness, and failing to meet these standards can lead to partial or full bond deductions.

Our bond cleaning services take the hassle out of this process by covering everything needed for a smooth handover. We offer a detailed and systematic cleaning that leaves no corner untouched, helping you fulfill your end-of-lease obligations with ease.

Comprehensive Bond Cleaning Checklist

Our bond cleaning checklist is designed to address all the critical areas required for a successful inspection. Our experienced team will:

  • Kitchen Cleaning: Degrease and sanitize all surfaces, including countertops, splashbacks, sinks, and taps. We thoroughly clean inside and outside of cabinets, drawers, and cupboards, as well as your oven, stovetop, range hood, and dishwasher. Every appliance is left gleaming and free from grime.
  • Bathroom and Toilet Cleaning: Scrub and disinfect showers, bathtubs, sinks, vanities, mirrors, and tiles. We remove soap scum, watermarks, and mold, ensuring all fixtures and fittings shine. Toilets are thoroughly sanitized, leaving them spotless and fresh.
  • Living Areas and Bedrooms: Dust all surfaces, including shelves, light fixtures, switches, and skirting boards. We vacuum and mop floors, remove cobwebs, and clean any marks from walls where possible, leaving the spaces tidy and presentable.
  • Window and Glass Cleaning: Our bond cleaning service includes interior window cleaning, including glass panels, tracks, and frames. We ensure windows are streak-free, allowing natural light to flow into every room.
  • Carpet Cleaning: Our carpet cleaning (available as an additional service) uses professional-grade equipment to remove stains, dirt, and allergens, revitalizing carpets for a fresh appearance.
  • Additional Areas: We also take care of additional areas such as balconies, patios, and garages upon request, leaving no area overlooked.
