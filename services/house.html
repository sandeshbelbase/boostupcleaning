House cleaning

Maintaining a clean and organized home can be time-consuming and exhausting. Our professional house cleaning services make it easy to enjoy a spotless, fresh environment without the hassle. Whether you need a one-time deep clean, regular maintenance, or move-in/move-out cleaning, our team is here to meet all your cleaning needs with precision and care.

Complete House Cleaning Solutions

We understand that every home is unique, which is why we offer customized cleaning plans tailored to your preferences and schedule. From kitchens and bathrooms to bedrooms and living spaces, we provide thorough cleaning for every area of your home.

General House Cleaning

Our general house cleaning service keeps your home looking its best with regular cleaning of high-traffic areas and common spaces. Our general cleaning includes:

  • Dusting: We dust all surfaces, including furniture, shelves, and decor, to remove dust and allergens.
  • Vacuuming and Mopping: We vacuum and mop floors to ensure they are spotless and free from dirt and debris.
  • Surface Wiping: We clean countertops, tables, and other surfaces to remove fingerprints, smudges, and spills.
  • Mirror and Glass Cleaning: We clean mirrors and glass surfaces for a streak-free, polished finish.

Kitchen Cleaning

Our kitchen cleaning services ensure this essential space is hygienic and sparkling. We tackle grease, grime, and other buildup with targeted cleaning, including:

  • Appliance Cleaning: We clean the exterior of appliances, such as the refrigerator, oven, and microwave, removing stains and buildup.
  • Countertops and Cabinets: We wipe down countertops and cabinet doors to eliminate grease and residue.
  • Sink and Faucet Cleaning: We scrub the sink and polish faucets to remove water stains and leave them shining.
  • Floor and Tile Cleaning: We mop floors and clean tile surfaces, ensuring your kitchen is spotless from top to bottom.

Bathroom Cleaning

Our bathroom cleaning service provides a deep clean to maintain a sanitary and fresh space. Our bathroom cleaning includes:

  • Shower and Tub Scrubbing: We clean and scrub the shower, tub, and tiles to remove soap scum, mildew, and stains.
  • Toilet Cleaning: We thoroughly clean and disinfect the toilet for optimal hygiene.
  • Sink and Mirror Cleaning: We clean sinks, polish faucets, and provide streak-free mirror cleaning for a polished look.
  • Floor and Surface Cleaning: We mop floors and wipe down surfaces, leaving the bathroom spotless and fresh.

Bedroom and Living Area Cleaning

Our bedroom and living area cleaning service ensures these relaxing spaces are clean, comfortable, and inviting. Our service includes:

  • Dusting and Surface Wiping: We dust furniture, shelves, and decor to remove dust and allergens.
  • Floor Cleaning: We vacuum carpets, rugs, and hard floors, keeping them clean and fresh.
  • Bed Making (Optional): We can make beds and arrange bedding for a tidy, welcoming look.
  • Upholstery Cleaning (Optional): We can vacuum and spot-clean upholstered furniture, leaving it looking refreshed.
