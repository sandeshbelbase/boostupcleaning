About Us

Boostup Cleaning Service is your dedicated partner for keeping homes, offices, and commercial spaces clean and well-maintained, providing a professional touch to every service. Established in 2022 as a sole-trader business, Boostup Cleaning Service operates across Dubbo and surrounding areas, delivering reliable and high-quality cleaning solutions. Boostup specialises in deep cleaning, end-of-lease cleans, builders/final cleans, as well as window and office cleaning. Every job is personally completed using eco-friendly products and modern equipment, ensuring thorough, hygienic, and dependable results. Flexible to meet specific client requirements, Boostup also offers after-hours services when needed for an additional cost. Attention to detail, consistency, and client satisfaction are at the heart of every service, making Boostup Cleaning Service a trusted choice for residential and commercial clients alike. With a commitment to quality and professionalism, Boostup continues to build strong relationships and deliver exceptional cleaning experiences for every client.
